Our Vision

To celebrate the vibrant heritage of Indian ethnic wear, making it accessible and stylish for every generation. We believe in blending tradition with modernity, empowering artisans, and bringing joy to our customers through authentic fashion.

Our Journey

Rangrathay began as a dream nurtured by Nakul, a broadcasting engineer whose zeal for entrepreneurship grew after years of dedicated work in his field. Driven by a vision for something of his own, Nakul set out to create a brand that celebrates Indian heritage and craftsmanship. His clarity and passion soon inspired Priya, who left her job as a teacher to join him on this journey. Together, they combined their unique experiences and values to build Rangrathay—a place where tradition meets innovation, and every customer is part of the story. Our commitment remains: to curate high-quality, affordable ethnic wear and support the communities behind every thread. Join us as we continue to weave dreams and celebrate culture, one outfit at a time.
